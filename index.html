How to export from Square
  1. Go to squareup.com → sign in → click Reports in the left sidebar
  2. Click Sales Summary — NOT Transactions, NOT Payroll
  3. Set your date range to the event day(s)
  4. Scroll down to the Items section → click Export as CSV
  5. Upload that file here — it should have columns like Item, Qty, Gross Sales
⚠️ Don't upload the Transactions CSV or the full Sales Summary PDF — those won't match item names correctly.
